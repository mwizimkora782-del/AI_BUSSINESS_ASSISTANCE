# AI Business Assistant

AI Business Assistant is an intelligent AI-powered platform designed to help businesses automate customer interaction, reservations, marketing, and business management.

The system is designed for restaurants, cafes, hotels, salons, retail stores, and other growing businesses.

---

# Vision

Our vision is to help businesses improve customer experience and increase growth using artificial intelligence and automation.

---

# Features

## Customer Support
- AI-powered customer conversations
- Instant responses
- 24/7 availability

## Reservations & Booking
- Table reservations
- Appointment scheduling
- Automatic confirmations

## Order Management
- Online ordering
- Product recommendations
- Delivery support

## AI Marketing
- Promotions and discounts
- Customer engagement
- Personalized recommendations

## Analytics Dashboard
- Sales reports
- Customer insights
- Business performance tracking
